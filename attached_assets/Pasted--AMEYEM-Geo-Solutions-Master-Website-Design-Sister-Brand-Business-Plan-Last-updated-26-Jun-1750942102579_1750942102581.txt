## 1 • Corporate Website (ameyem.com) — Polished Design Blueprint

### 1.1 Primary Goals

1. **Demonstrate technical leadership** in Oil & Gas geoscience software, AI/ML services, and consulting.
2. Provide **clear conversion paths** for (a) B2B corporate decision‑makers and (b) local parents seeking tech education for children.
3. Act as the **umbrella hub** that routes visitors to sister brands (StoryLikho, Chess99, Sambandhalu).

### 1.2 Visual & UX Guidelines

| Element           | Recommendation                                                                                                                                                                      |
| ----------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Style**         | Clean, modern, professional. White & charcoal base; accent with deep ocean blue (#004a7c) and energy orange (#ff7a00). Rounded‑edge cards, light drop‑shadows, generous whitespace. |
| **Fonts**         | Headings – *Poppins SemiBold*; Body – *Inter Regular*.                                                                                                                              |
| **Imagery**       | High‑resolution seismic sections, code‑snippet overlays, classroom photos for Kids section. Use subtle animated SVG waves in hero background.                                       |
| **Accessibility** | WCAG 2.2 AA contrast, keyboard‑navigable menus, alt‑text.                                                                                                                           |
| **Performance**   | Serve static assets from CDN, lazy‑load images, implement defer/async for scripts.                                                                                                  |

## 3 • Integrated Business Plan (Three‑Year Horizon)

### 3.1 Executive Summary

Ameyem Geo Solutions Pvt Ltd (“Ameyem”) delivers niche geoscience software and AI consulting while incubating consumer‑facing EdTech and Content SaaS products. The umbrella model maximises IP reuse and cross‑domain data expertise.

### 3.2 Market Analysis

| Segment                         | TAM      | Pain Points                            | Competitor Gap                                           | Ameyem Advantage                                            |
| ------------------------------- | -------- | -------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------- |
| O\&G Digital Transformation     |  USD 4 B | Manual log processing, low ML adoption | Big vendors (Schlumberger) too costly for midsize fields | Agile custom tools, field‑tested geophysicist founder       |
| Technical Writing SaaS          |  USD 2 B | Idea‑to‑draft friction                 | Generic AI writers lack long‑form structure              | Geoscience‑grade semantic models, credit + subscription mix |
| Kids STEM Education (Hyderabad) | ₹ 500 Cr | Quality curriculum, trust              | Tuition centres focus on academics, not tech             | IIT‑educated mentor, small‑batch hands‑on courses           |

### 3.3 Products & Revenue Streams

1. **AutoSplicing Tool** — one‑off licence + annual maintenance; pay‑per‑well model.
2. **Geoscience AI Consulting** — project fees + success‑fee kicker (e.g. % uplift on production).
3. **StoryLikho** — freemium (10 k words/month) then ₹ 499/mo or pay‑as‑you‑go credits.
4. **Chittibadi** — ad‑supported community, premium creator tools ₹ 99/mo.
5. **Kids Courses** — ₹ 12 000 per 12‑week cohort (max 5 students).
6. **Chess99** — free play, premium analytics ₹ 199/yr.

### 3.4 Go‑to‑Market & Marketing Plan

* **B2B**
  • Publish white‑papers (Resources blog)
  • Exhibit at Urja Varta & GEO India
  • Targeted LinkedIn ads (reservoir managers)
  • Case‑study webinars (Cairn, Selan).
* **B2C**
  • Instagram reels of classroom builds
  • Local school workshops
  • Referral discounts (₹ 1 000 off per friend)
  • Cross‑promo banners among sister sites.
